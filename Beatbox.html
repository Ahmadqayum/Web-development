What Is BeatBox?

Beatboxing (also beat boxing) is a form of vocal percussion primarily
involving the art of mimicking drum machines (typically a TR-808),
using one's mouth, lips, tongue, and voice. It may also involve
vocal imitation of turntablism, and other musical instruments.
